AN ODE TO CN VIDEO
Q. Why did you revive Cartoon Network Video?
A. For years now, I've had this specific memory of sitting in front of my father's computer, eating a toaster pastry, and viewing Urban Ed, and a couple episodes of Totally Spies. I wanted to get as close as I could to that experience again. Where nothing mattered. In a world of digital streaming and enshitification, I wanted to build a tool that allows YOU to control your cartoon library in only the most suitable fashion.

Q. Where can I talk more about this project?
A. I'm reopening an old Discord server I had dedicated to preserving the CN Video Podcast. Feel free to join @ https://discord.gg/baB6e985sd

GENERAL
Q. What is Cartoon Network Video?
A. Cartoon Network Video is the awesome broadband destination that lets fans immerse themselves in the world of cartoons, from well-known favorites to brand-new animation.

Q. Is it free?
A. Yes! Cartoon Network Video is absolutely free to use.

Q. Why are there ads on Cartoon Network Video?
A. In order to offer all this incredible content free of charge, Cartoon Network Video uses ad revenue to pay the bills.
CONTENT
Q. What can I watch on Cartoon Network Video?
A. Cartoon Network Video offers full-length episodes, animated shorts, special presentations and tons of bonus content. Basically, it puts cartoon fans in command of their very own animation headquarters.

Q. Will Cartoon Network Video be adding more shows to its lineup?
A. Yes! Moving forward, Cartoon Network Video will continue to add more new shows as well as lots of extra features. It's all part of Cartoon Network Video's goal to bring you the best cartoons anywhere and make it easy to watch whatever you want whenever you want.

Q. How long are episodes available on the site?
A. Individual episodes are available on Cartoon Network Video for a few weeks at a time. You can watch the episodes as many times as you want during that period, absolutely free.

Q. How can I find out more information about a show?
A. Just visit http://www.CartoonNetwork.com to find out more about the shows on Cartoon Network Video.
USING THE SITE
Q. How do I watch an episode?
A. To start an episode, click on the thumbnail image next to the episode title. To choose a particular scene from that episode, click on the down arrow in the bottom right corner of the episode cell and select a scene from the mini-thumbnails that drop down.

Q. I scrolled to the bottom of an episode list — how do I get to the next page of episodes?
A. To get to the next page of episodes, click on the right arrow at the bottom of the navigation panel or type a page number in the box and click "Go."

Q. Can I change the size of the video?
A. Of course! You can click the "Full" button to expand the video to full-screen size.

Q. How do I share a video with a friend?
A. There are a couple of ways to share a video using the icons in the bottom left-hand corner of the page. You can either copy and paste a link (Link to This Video) or e-mail the link directly to a friend (Send to Friend).

Q. What is "Comments"?
A. Comments is the section where users can submit comments or questions about shows, individual episodes, the site itself or anything else related to Cartoon Network Video. Selected comments will be displayed here on the site.

Q. How do I send Cartoon Network Video my own comments?
A. You can let us know what you think by clicking on "Comments," typing in a user name and your comment and clicking the "Submit" button.
SEARCH
Q. How do I search for something?
A. Click on the "Search" button at the top of the page and enter a word into the space provided. You can enter show names, character names or words from episode titles to find what you want.
MY FAVORITES
Q. What is "My Favorites"?
A. My Favorites is an easy way to build a list of all your favorite episodes and watch them whenever. You can add and remove as many videos as you want, as often as you want.

Q. What happens to My Favorites when I close Cartoon Network Video?
A. Nothing! It saves all of your favorite episodes, even if you turn the computer off and on.

Q. How do I add a video to My Favorites?
A. Click on the "plus button" underneath each episode title.

Q. How do I remove a video from My Favorites?
A. Click on the "minus button" underneath each episode title.

Q. One of the episodes disappeared from My Favorites! What happened?
A. Just like Video On Demand, episodes are available on the site for a few weeks at a time. When a video leaves the site, it leaves your My Favorites list as well.
TECHNICAL
Q. I can't see Cartoon Network Video on my computer! What's wrong?
A. To run Cartoon Network Video, your system must meet the following requirements:

  • You must have a high-speed connection (DSL or Cable modem).
The minimum system configuration:

  • Operating System: Windows 2000 SP4+, Windows XP, Mac OS X
  • Browser: Internet Explorer 5.5+ or Firefox 1.5+
  • Flash Player: Macromedia Flash 8

Q. Can I watch Cartoon Network Video in Firefox?
A. Yes, Firefox is now supported for Cartoon Network Video.

Q. My video says "Buffering." What does that mean?
A. Buffering just means your video is in the process of loading.
RSS
Q. What is RSS?
A. RSS stands for Really Simple Syndication and is just a high-tech way of making a newsletter. You can get instant updates by subscribing to an RSS feed.

Q. How do I subscribe to an RSS feed?
A. Just click the "RSS Feed" button, copy and paste the RSS feed into your RSS reader, and you're good to go!

Q. What is Livewire?
A. Livewire is the coolest way to hear about all the awesome new stuff on Cartoon Network Video! Just download and install Livewire on your desktop, and you'll get a message any time Cartoon Network Video is updated.
